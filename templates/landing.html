Welcome to TopSpot!

TopSpot can be used to explore the top streamed songs of Spotify (in 2023). You can take our short questionnaire to see which of the top streamed songs you might enjoy.

Spotify's song stats

Spotify uses "song stats" to break a song down into quantifiable metrics that could be relevant to a listeners taste. The stats used in this project are as follows:

  • Danceability:
    Describes how suitable a track is for dancing.
  • Valence:
    Describes how suitable a track is for dancing.
  • Energy:
    Describes how suitable a track is for dancing.
  • Acousticness:
    Describes how suitable a track is for dancing.
  • Instrumentalness:
    Describes how suitable a track is for dancing.
  • Liveness:
