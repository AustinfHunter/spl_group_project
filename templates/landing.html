Welcome to TopSpot!

TopSpot can be used to explore the top streamed songs of Spotify (in 2023). You can take our short questionnaire to see which of the top streamed songs you might enjoy.

Spotify's song stats

Spotify uses "song stats" to break a song down into quantifiable metrics that could be relevant to a listeners taste. The stats used in this project are as follows:

  • Danceability:
    A measure of how suitable a track is for dancing.
  • Valence:
    A measure of how positive or "cheery" a track is.
  • Energy:
    A measure of the perceived level of energy of the track.
  • Acousticness:
    A measure of how much of the track consists of acoustic instruments.
  • Instrumentalness:
    A measure of how much of the track is instrumental.
  • Liveness:
    A measure of perceived "liveness," which is an estimation of whether the track was performed live or in studio
  • Speechiness:
    A measure of how much of the track consists of spoken words.

The Data
